Our Story

Who We Are
& Why We're Different

EPCVIP was built on a simple thesis: if you combine deep financial industry expertise with proprietary technology and own the full operation from traffic to lead, you win. That's exactly what we built.

Our founder Valeriy Zeltser, with a background in economics, mathematics, and risk management, personally built V1.0 of our platform — producing 25%+ conversion increases for clients from day one. Today we generate tens of thousands of leads daily across every major financial vertical, with 74 experts operating across 4 continents.

  • Technology-first company, not a traditional agency
  • Largest direct publishing lead provider in the personal loan space
  • Named Top Vendor by CFO Tech Outlook & Financial Services Review
  • Active in the industry at Affiliate Summit, Lend360, and LeadsCon
